Supporting Teachers of Exceptional Students

We're dedicated to providing ongoing, individualized supports for educators to deliver AT-based instructions for their diverse learners.

Empower

We empower teachers to become self-directed learners who can decide and control their own path of professional learning. AT corner accommodates all topics related to the integration of ATs to meet teachers’ diverse needs.

Collaborate

Teaching should not be an isolated practice! We connect teachers to a community of learners who share the same problem or learning goal. Teachers can ask questions, seek answers and share their experiences at the convenience of their own.
